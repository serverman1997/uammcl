Know About Our Vision 2035

Unati Behan strengthens Bharat by empowering communities.

Unati Behan represents the empowered spirit of the middle-class Bharatiya Nari, dedicated to strengthening Bharat by creating livelihood opportunities for millions and supporting Kisan Bhai.

Our Objective

  • Sustainable Living – Promoting eco-friendly products for a healthier planet and future generations.
  • Supporting Local Farmers – Strengthening rural communities by sourcing directly from hardworking farmers.
  • Quality You Can Trust – Delivering carefully selected and rigorously tested products for your family’s safety.

Get in touch

UAMMCL is a proud extension of Unati, carrying forward its vision for Bharat.

More than 21+ years experience

We strive to enhance the capacity of rural communities to achieve a sustainable livelihood. Our structure consists of field-based groups, each comprising three to five dynamic young professionals, led by an experienced mid-career coordinator and supported by a team of expert integrators. Hundreds of young professionals are actively engaged in remote villages across India, working closely with local communities in some of the most underprivileged states.

UAMMCL: Empowering Bharat, Transforming Lives

UAMMCL is committed to building a self-reliant Bharat by empowering women, supporting farmers, and ensuring the well-being of consumers. Through sustainable livelihood opportunities, fair trade practices, and strong cooperative networks.

Our Objective

  • Mass Livelihood Generation for Women – Creating opportunities and financial independence for women.
  • Health and Well-being of Consumers – Ensuring safe, nutritious, and high-quality products for every household.
  • Assured Marketing Platform for Farmers – Providing farmers with a reliable and fair marketplace.
  • Cooperation Among Cooperatives – Strengthening collaboration for sustainable growth and development.
