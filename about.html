Vision

To create a sustainable and inclusive supply chain by fostering the Indian ethos of cooperation, ensuring prosperity for every stakeholder, and embracing the spirit of Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam.

Mission

  • Empower farmers by doubling their income through fair and transparent trade practices.
  • Promote large-scale livelihood generation with a special focus on women empowerment.
  • Deliver high-quality, affordable products to consumers, maintaining ethical and sustainable production.
  • Strengthen the cooperative culture to build a self-reliant and globally connected Bharat.
