Unati Drone Initiative

More than 21+ year experience

Unati is taking a step forward with the Unati Drone Initiative—a mission to empower farmers through cutting-edge drone technology.

By leveraging advanced aerial monitoring, automated spraying, and real-time data analytics, we are enhancing crop health, resource efficiency, and productivity.

With precision agriculture, farmers can make smarter, data-driven decisions, reduce costs, and boost yields—ensuring a sustainable and prosperous future for agriculture.

Project Amudha

This flagship project was initiated in J&K to empower the youth and to generate employment for women. ensuring they are processed efficiently and marketed effectively. Decentralizing processing activities ensures high-quality standards and minimizes waste.

Decentralizing processing activities plays a big role in maintaining high-quality standards and reducing waste. When processing is done closer to the source, it helps in better handling of raw materials, leading to fresher and more valuable products. This also boosts local economies by creating jobs and increasing income for small-scale producers.

Our Objective

  • Empowers youth and creates job opportunities for women.
  • Ensures high-quality standards and reduces waste.
  • Supports local economies by creating more employment.
